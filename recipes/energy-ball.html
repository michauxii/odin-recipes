Easy Energy Balls

Original recipe here

Description

My friend showed me this recipe and I'm obsessed with them. These are quick easy to make, and they are delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Instant Oats
  • 2/3 Cup Flaked Coconut
  • 1/2 Cup Peanut Butter
  • 1/2 Cup Flax Seeds
  • 1/2 Cup Chocolate Chips
  • 1/4 Cup Honey

Steps

  1. Stir oats, coconut, peanut butter, flax seeds, chocolate chips, and honey together in a large bowl until well-combined. Roll into golf ball-size balls.
  2. That's it! Serve chilled, if desired.
